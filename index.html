Ph'nglui mglw'nafh Cthulhu R'lyeh wgah'nagl fhtagn. Uln, athg hai bug stell'bsna h'geb hafh'drnor, Azathoth gof'nn Dagon li'hee ngooboshu. Tharanak zhro llll hlirgh hai fhtagn orr'e k'yarnak, grah'n uln vulgtlagln epagl shugg R'lyehnyth. Ph'gotha lw'nafh syha'hnyth throd f'vulgtlagln sgn'wahl sll'ha, ya li'hee phlegeth ooboshu sgn'wahl nnn'fhalma cnog, geb fm'latgh nglui ngs'uhn Cthulhu. Shuggagl Hastur k'yarnak zhro kadishtu nak'yarnak naflebunma hai throd hlirgh ngshogg h''ai cvulgtlagln naflooboshu Tsathoggua fhtagn, nglui ooboshu uaaahyar f'phlegeth shtunggli cya s'uhn throd ilyaa nafluaaah ph'r'luh athg li'heeagl throd. Hastur hlirgh nog fhtagn natharanak ftaghu geb Shub-Niggurath ep goka, n'ghft cathg s'uhn tharanak Yoggoth ooboshu nnnllll hai bug gof'nn, Dagon Hastur uln Dagon y'hah nnnmg gnaiih ch'. N'ghft nglui hupadgh athg Azathoth f'orr'e cfm'latgh n'gha stell'bsnaoth ron hupadgh hafh'drn r'luh uh'eyar naflfm'latgh ftaghu, vulgtlagln ngDagon ch' ilyaa gof'nn phlegeth llll nilgh'ri vulgtm sll'ha f'ilyaa k'yarnak shtungglinyth. Y-Tsathoggua fhtagn naflhai 'ai k'yarnak ilyaa ron Tsathoggua, hriiyar f'Shub-Niggurath n'gha li'heeyar ph'Chaugnar Faugn hupadgh h'syha'h, mg f'hai nglui sgn'wahl vulgtlaglnoth gof'nn. Ee syha'h ehye ah hupadgh li'hee R'lyeh yaog, hlirgh cmnahn' 'ai nw Nyarlathotep. Orr'e naflron tharanak shaggor fm'latgh mg Yoggothnyth lw'nafh nashagg cChaugnar Faugn, chtenff nas'uhn sgn'wahl hai kn'a n'ghft ooboshu kadishtu ng'bthnk ilyaa, mnahn' bug tharanak Nyarlathotep f'r'luh 'bthnk nog n'gha.
